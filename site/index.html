Our Menu

Chicken

Enjoy our Tsukune - chicken cakes with teriyaki sauce. Small chicken cakes, about the size of meatballs. We also serve Yakitori, Japanese-style chicken kebabs. All the chicken used in our recipes is certified organic and free range.

Beef

For a special occasion Yakiniku is the perfect choice. Thinly sliced beef, fresh vegetables and tofu cooked on a hot plate at your table. All your guests can participate in cooking their own food.

Sushi

Different varieties of fresh seafood (squid, prawns, octupus, tuna or salmon) pressed on rice. Try our specially shaped sushi or sushi wrapped in seaweed or tofu. All served with sushi vinegar.
